About Me:

Hello World! My name is Everett Diuguid. I am an aspiring coder/web-developer currently enrolled in The Coding Boot Camp at UNC-Chapel Hill. I am an Alumni of Appalachian State University with degrees in History (BA) and Spanish Language and Literature (BA).

Prior to starting my journey through coding, I worked for over a decade in the service and hospitality industry, gaining a love for the culinary arts while building a strong work ethic with a primary focus on attention to detail. I spend my free time outside; tending to vegetables in the garden, hiking or biking through North Carolina's amazing state parks, or at the computer building up my coding abilities. I have a strong passion for live music, photography, and all things food.
